How to cite
===========
.. meta::
    :description lang=en:
        If you use multiphenicsx in your work, please cite our website

If you use **multiphenicsx** in your work, please cite the `multiphenicsx website <https://multiphenics.github.io/>`__ as

.. code-block:: bibtex

    @misc{multiphenicsx,
      author = {Ballarin, Francesco},
      title  = {{multiphenicsx} - easy prototyping of multiphysics problems in {FEniCSx}},
      year   = {2020},
      url    = {https://multiphenics.github.io/}
    }
