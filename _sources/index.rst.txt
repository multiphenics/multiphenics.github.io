Description
-----------

.. image:: _static/images/multiphenicsx-logo.png
    :height: 150px
    :width: 150px
    :align: right
    :alt: multiphenicsx

**multiphenicsx** is a python library that aims at providing tools in **FEniCSx** for an easy prototyping of multiphysics problems on conforming meshes. In particular, it facilitates the definition of subdomain/boundary restricted variables.

**multiphenicsx** is the successor to **multiphenics**, a library with similar goals that used to target **FEniCS**.

Authors
------------------------

**multiphenicsx** is currently developed and maintained at `Università Cattolica del Sacro Cuore <https://www.unicatt.it/>`__ by `Dr. Francesco Ballarin <https://www.francescoballarin.it>`__.

------------------------

   :widths: 10 90

   * - 2016

   * - 2016-2021
     - Financial support of the `AROMA-CFD H2020 ERC CoG project <https://people.sissa.it/~grozza/aroma-cfd/>`__ (PI: `Prof. Gianluigi Rozza <https://people.sissa.it/~grozza/>`__, host: `SISSA mathLab <http://mathlab.sissa.it/>`__) for the consolidation of **multiphenics** development.
   * - 2020
     - Early development of **multiphenicsx** begins at `SISSA mathLab <http://mathlab.sissa.it/>`__.
   * - 2021
     - The development of **multiphenicsx** moves to `Università Cattolica del Sacro Cuore <https://www.unicatt.it/>`__.
   * - 2022
     - **multiphenics** is retired. Users are encouraged to move to **multiphenicsx** forthwith.
